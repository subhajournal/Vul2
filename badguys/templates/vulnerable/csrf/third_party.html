CSRF from a Third-Party Site

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

As an evil genius you are interested in tricking unsuspecting users into giving you gift cards. We're going to simulate an attack from an innocent-looking but secretly evil third-party site by enticing the victim to gaze at adorable cat photos.

Open up the template for this page -- badguys/templates/vulnerable/csrf/third_party.html -- in your editor and create an innocent-looking form that will POST data to the vulnerable gift card form. There will be an HTML comment inviting you to add the form.

  • Your email address is evil@evil.com
  • You want $100 giftcards (the field expects an integer)
  • All inputs (except the submit) should be hidden
  • Your submit button should say View Photos because that looks innocent
